About Me

About Me

I am a Nashville native who has recently moved back home. I have spent most of my professional career working in the service industry. While I love the fast pace, and camaraderie it instills, I began looking into programming from a desire to create new avenues of positive change in my community. I came to love not only the idea of what I could do with the ability to connect people but also programming itself after babbling into JavaScript. Now, I can’t imagine another career path for myself.

While I have always been interested in technology, like many of my generation my initial interests rarely strayed beyond the value outside of entertainment or personal comfort. It wasn't until the last few years that my interests deviated from looking into code to modify games and instead viewed coding as a vehicle to better myself and my community. My initial tentative steps into software development began when I enrolled in a short, in person foundations program at Nashville Software School in the winter of 2019. From the first day of covering JavaScript I was enamored with both the aspect of what could be accomplished with software development and the joy of working with code. I am currently enrolled in a full-time six month software development bootcamp at Nashville Software School.

Experience

Professional Experience

Over the last decade I have worked for Publix Supermarkets in various roles, from customer service staff to grocery replenishment specialist to deli manager. The only department or role that I have not worked in or at least covered during labor shortages is working as a pharmacy technician. Throughout my time at Publix, I grew to love and admire the culture that emphasized service to both the customer and Publix associates. It is something I will carry with me all through my life. The majority of my time at Publix has been in positions of team leadership. In addition to the obvious roles of assistant and deli manager, while working as a customer service staff or grocery replenishment specialist I lead smaller groups with varying degrees of managerial oversight from check-ins at start of shift to quarterly reviews. This time in leadership not only taught me the skills required for business management and team leadership, but it also taught me the importance of team culture regardless of an individual’s position in the team. My employment with Publix focused on the following skill set: teamwork, team building, team management, training, customer service, merchandising, and building a results oriented work environment.

Currently, I am enrolled in an intensive full-time six month software development bootcamp with concerted learning in both individual and team projects. This course began as an in person bootcamp, and has since moved to remote work with the Covid-19 pandemic. Group projects have focused on working in teams using the SCRUM methodology in pair-programming sessions using VS Code and VS Live Share. The tech stack includes: C#/.NET, React.js, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Flexbox, Git, GitHub, and SQL fundamentals
